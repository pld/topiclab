The Ureport data was divided into 5 topics (number not selected for any speical reason) based on the LDA algorithm, which uses word co-occurence.

Note the bars are not normalized by response occurence, and they should be.

The bar charts show, from topic 1 on the left to topic 5 on the right, the number of times a response from that district was assigned to this topic.

The tag clouds, below, show the first 10 unique words by frequency for each topic scaled based on number of occurences in the topic.

Note that LDA is a stochastic algorithm, another run will produce different topics, different unique words, and different frequencies for those unique words.
